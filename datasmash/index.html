Case Study: How Jayant Mine Reduced Its Carbon Footprint

Overview: Discover how Jayant Mine, one of India’s largest coal mining operations, reduced its carbon emissions by 30% using our platform. The mine adopted our comprehensive carbon management tools to monitor, reduce, and offset emissions.

The Challenge

Faced with high emissions from transportation and mining equipment, [Coal Mine Name] needed a solution to measure their carbon footprint and offer practical pathways for reduction. They sought cleaner energy sources, optimized operations, and aimed for productivity without compromising sustainability.

Our Solution

By utilizing our platform, the mine was able to:

  • Accurately measure carbon emissions from various activities.
  • Implement renewable energy strategies like solar and wind energy.
  • Offset remaining emissions by planting 500 hectares of trees and purchasing carbon credits.
  • Adopt electric vehicles to lower diesel emissions.

Results

  • 30% reduction in carbon emissions.
  • 25% of energy consumption offset through renewable sources.
  • Rs 5,00,000 saved annually through carbon credits.
  • Increased operational efficiency, reducing energy costs and boosting sustainability.
